Data Deletion Instructions — ToyCalc

This page explains how users of the ToyCalc app (developed by Maicon Silva / NewCore Technologies) can request the deletion of their data.

What data does ToyCalc collect?

ToyCalc is a simple calculator app for children. It does not require account creation, login, or the input of any personal information by the user. The only data processed is the device's Advertising ID, which is used exclusively by the Google AdMob SDK to display ads within the app. ToyCalc does not directly store, access, or retain this identifier on its own servers.

How to request data deletion

Since ToyCalc does not collect or store personal data on its own servers, there is no user account or stored profile to delete. If you have any privacy-related questions or wish to confirm what limited data may be associated with your device through third-party ad services, you can request information or deletion by contacting the developer directly:

  • Developer: Maicon Silva (NewCore Technologies)
  • Email: newcore.apps@gmail.com

What happens after a request

The developer will respond to your request within one month, as required by applicable data protection law (such as the GDPR). Any data that can be identified and is under the developer's control will be deleted or confirmed as not held.

Third-party services

ToyCalc uses Google AdMob to display advertising. To manage or reset your Advertising ID, or to opt out of personalized advertising at the device level, please refer to your device's privacy settings or to Google's Ads Settings.
